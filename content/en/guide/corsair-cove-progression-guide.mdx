<Section icon={Shield} title="Crew Cohesion and Unity Management">

In Corsair Cove, your crew is not just a workforce; they are a volatile group of individuals held together by shared goals. This is represented by the Cohesion mechanic. Cohesion measures the strength of your crew's unity. If this bar drops to zero, the dream of your pirate empire ends in a game over as your crew abandons ship.

Maintaining high cohesion is vital because it acts as a secondary currency. High unity allows you to perform strategic actions and make tough leadership decisions that would otherwise cause a mutiny. Sometimes, you may even have to sacrifice individual pirates or resources to ensure the collective stays together.

| Cohesion Level | Status Effect | Gameplay Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **High (80-100)** | United Force | Bonus to work speed, Strategic actions available |
| **Medium (40-79)** | Stable Crew | Standard performance, No immediate risks |
| **Low (1-39)** | Rising Tension | Frequent conflicts, Reduced efficiency |
| **Zero (0)** | Abandonment | Game Over, Crew leaves the island |

<Callout type="warning" title="Mutiny Risk">
Never let your Cohesion linger in the low zone. One bad event or a failed decision can trigger a downward spiral that is nearly impossible to recover from without significant sacrifices.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Building and Logistics">

As your island grows, the walking paths of your pirates become a bottleneck. A common mistake is placing housing far away from the workplace. While pirates only strictly "need" a roof over their heads, placing their homes in the middle of Skull Rock or on cliffside terraces near their jobs will drastically improve your resource flow.

| Building Category | Placement Tip | Resource Needed |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Housing** | Can be placed anywhere; prioritize verticality | Wood, Fabric |
| **Workshops** | Place near storage depots | Wood, Iron |
| **Shipyards** | Must be on the shoreline | Planks, Tar, Rope |
| **Storage** | Centralize between production and docks | Wood |
