<Section icon={Swords} title="The Safespot Strategy">

The core of any **corsair cove walkthrough** is the safespotting technique. Ogress Warriors are massive, taking up a 2x2 tile space, which makes them very easy to trap behind environmental obstacles. There are two primary safespots in the main dungeon chamber that allow you to fire at the warriors while they are unable to reach you with their melee attacks.

### Primary Safespot: The Southern Pillar
Located near the entrance, there is a large stalagmite. By standing on the eastern side of this pillar, you can pull an Ogress Warrior toward you. It will get stuck on the corner of the pillar, allowing you to attack freely. 

### Secondary Safespot: The Bone Pile
Further into the dungeon, there is a pile of bones and debris. By standing behind this debris, you can safely attack the Ogresses that spawn in the northern section of the room. This spot is often less crowded than the southern pillar.

| Safespot Location | Max Targets | Crowding Level | Difficulty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Southern Pillar** | 2 | High | Easy |
| **Northern Debris** | 1 | Medium | Moderate |
| **Western Wall** | 1 | Low | Hard |

<Callout type="info" title="Aggro Mechanics">
Ogress Warriors are aggressive to players within a certain combat level range. If they stop attacking you automatically, simply run out of the room and back in to "reset" their aggression, or manually click them to continue farming.
</Callout>
