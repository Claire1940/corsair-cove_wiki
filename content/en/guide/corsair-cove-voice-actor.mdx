### Rambullion, the Tavernkeep
Voiced with a deep, gravelly tone, Rambullion (formerly known as Tadhg MacMhuircheartaich) represents the cautious wisdom of a survivor. Having witnessed the fall of a previous pirate haven, his performance conveys a sense of weariness mixed with quiet hope. His lines are delivered slowly, reflecting a character who watches everyone in his tavern and speaks only when necessary.

### Teach, the Brute
Edward Teach, better known as Blackbeard, requires a powerful, intimidating vocal presence. The voice acting for Teach emphasizes his brutal nature and physical dominance. His commanding shouts and gravelly laughter reinforce his status as the last of the old guard, a pirate who believes that the cove needs a strong hand to survive the Crown's onslaught.

### Kanja, the Gambler
Kanja Shan's voice acting is smooth, melodic, and highly calculated. Rising from a wealthy spice merchant to a pirate sponsor, Kanja's lines are filled with confidence and charm. The performance captures the essence of a high-stakes gambler who is always planning his next move, even when facing extreme danger.

### Scarlet, the Fearless
Scarlet Drake's voice is full of fire and determination. Her performance features a distinct Irish accent, capturing her background as an orphan who fought her way to becoming a feared captain. Her voiced lines are energetic and passionate, emphasizing her pursuit of legendary status rather than mere coin.

### Honorata, the Adventurer
Honorata Van Horn represents the educated, rebellious spirit of the high seas. Rejecting her merchant-family future, her voice lines are delivered with a confident, articulate, yet daring tone. Her dialogue focuses heavily on exploration, navigation, and her obsession with hunting the ocean's mythical creatures.
