<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Gamepad Setup Guide">

If you find that your gamepad isn't being recognized immediately, or if the button prompts are not matching your device, follow these steps to optimize your **corsair cove controller support** settings.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enable Steam Input">
    Right-click the game in your Steam Library, select **Properties**, then navigate to the **Controller** tab. Set the override to **Enable Steam Input**. This ensures the game translates your inputs correctly regardless of the hardware brand.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select a Community Layout">
    Navigate to the **Controller Layout** section in the Steam Overlay. Search for "Corsair Cove Official" or top-rated community layouts that feature specialized radial menus for building categories.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Adjust Cursor Sensitivity">
    In the in-game settings, look for the **Controller Sensitivity** slider. Since building requires placing structures on narrow cliffs, a lower sensitivity often helps with precision placement of ladders and road systems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Calibrate Deadzones">
    To prevent "drift" when exploring the world map, calibrate your stick deadzones within the Steam Controller settings. This is especially important for older gamepads that might have slight mechanical wear.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Building Warning">
Placing buildings on steep cliffs can be finicky with a joystick. Use the **Snap-to-Grid** toggle (usually mapped to the Left Trigger) to ensure your Sawmills and Tents align perfectly with the road system.
</Callout>

</Section>
