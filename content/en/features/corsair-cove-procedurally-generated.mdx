<Section icon={Swords} title="Fleet Preparation and Naval Combat">

The world map is filled with **corsair cove procedurally generated** events that provide the resources your island might lack. From Smuggler Transports to Ancient Temple Expeditions, your fleet must be prepared for diverse challenges.

Naval combat in *Corsair Cove* is turn-based and relies on a combination of ship ratings, captain traits, and dice pools. Success is determined by matching the right ship to the right encounter.

| Ship Class | Health | Crew/Supplies | Best Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Landlubber** | 24 | 15 / 20 | Early-game scouting and basic raids. |
| **Steel Edge** | 88 | 15 / 22 | Mid-game combat and fortified targets. |
| **Iron Fist** | 65 | 12 / 18 | High-durability defensive escort. |

**Combat Flow Steps:**
1. **Read Objectives**: Identify if the goal is to destroy the enemy, close distance, or loot goods.
2. **Check Enemy Actions**: The opponent reveals its intent; choose to counter or push your objective.
3. **Manage Supplies**: Most Battle cards cost Supplies; use Support cards to recover them during the fight.
4. **Roll Dice Pools**: Higher results improve outcomes, but attackers and defenders can both resolve actions simultaneously.

<Callout type="tip" title="Captain Strategy">
Captains like "Teach" provide massive bonuses to specific playstyles. Teach's "Easy as Murder" ability allows you to chain extra dice as long as you keep dealing damage to enemy health. Match your captain to your ship's strongest ratings.
