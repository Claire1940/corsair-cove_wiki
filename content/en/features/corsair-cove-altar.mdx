<Section icon={Swords} title="The Corsair Cove Dungeon and Ogress Combat">

Immediately west of the main town lies the entrance to the **Corsair Cove Dungeon**. This is the premier destination for mid-level F2P combat training. The dungeon is inhabited by Ogress Warriors and Ogress Shamans, known for their valuable drop tables that include high-tier armor and runes.

**Ogress Drop Table Highlights:**
| Item | Rarity | Value / Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rune Full Helm** | Rare | High Alch / Combat |
| **Limpwurt Root** | Common | Herblore / Profit |
| **Shaman Mask** | Rare | Cosmetic / Collection |
| **Law/Nature Runes** | Uncommon | Utility / Profit |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Preparation">
    Bring food and ranged or magic gear, as Ogresses can be safely spotted behind rocks in the dungeon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Entrance">
    Locate the hole west of the main town. Ensure you have completed the necessary quest steps to avoid the "curse" dialogue.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Positioning">
    Use the stalagmites to block the Ogress Warriors' melee attacks. Shamans use magic, so high magic defense is recommended.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Danger Zone">
Ogress Shamans can hit accurately with magic. If you are low level, stay near the exits or use Protect from Magic if available.
</Callout>

</Section>
