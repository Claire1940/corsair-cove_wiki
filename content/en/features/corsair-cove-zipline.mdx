<Section icon={Target} title="Setup Guide: Placing Your First Zipline">

Implementing a **corsair cove zipline** requires careful planning. Unlike roads, ziplines have a maximum span and require clear line-of-sight between the start and end points. If a cliff outcropping blocks the cable, the building will be invalidated.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Resource Gap">
    Locate a high-altitude resource deposit, such as **Iron** or **Copper**, that is currently taking your fetchers too long to reach via stairs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear the Path">
    Demolish any obstructing tents or small production buildings that sit directly between the resource deposit and your central warehouse.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Place the Upper Anchor">
    Build the Zipline Station at the highest point. Ensure it has a road connection to the nearby mines or gatherers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Connect the Lower Anchor">
    Drag the connection tool to a lower distribution hub or pier. The UI will turn green when the distance and angle are optimal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Tip">
Place your lower zipline anchors directly adjacent to **Taverns**. This keeps your workers' spirits high as they can immediately access "bread and booze" after a long shift of transporting heavy materials from the peaks.
</Callout>

</Section>
