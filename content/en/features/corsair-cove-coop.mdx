- **Verticality** is key; build on cliff-faces and use ziplines to optimize your pirate harbor's efficiency.
- **Production chains** require careful resource management, ranging from basic stew to advanced ship components.

| **Warehouse** | Resource storage | Centralize for logistics |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Basic Needs">
    Build campfires and gather food immediately to prevent cohesion loss.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Establish Logistics">
    Connect your resource buildings to the Pirate Camp using roads and cliff paths.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Scale Up Production">
    Once stable, build sawmills and warehouses to prepare for ship construction.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Expand to New Islands">
    Use ships to raid nearby islands for resources you cannot produce at home.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Efficiency Alert">

<Section icon={Swords} title="Naval Combat and Exploration">

Naval combat in Corsair Cove is turn-based, requiring you to manage supplies and crew health. Every decision in the Seven Seas impacts your pirate legacy.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Combat Stats">
    - **Gun Damage**: Deals damage to enemy health.
    - **Boarding Damage**: Attacks enemy crew directly.
    - **Speed/Maneuverability**: Used to avoid incoming fire.
    - **Armor**: Reduces overall incoming damage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tactical Supplies">
    - Supplies act as your "action points."
    - Running out limits your combat options.
    - Replenishing supplies often requires sacrificing health or crew.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="success" title="Strategy">
Always assess the enemy ship's stats before engaging. If your ship is outclassed in gunpower, focus on boarding maneuvers to neutralize their crew and minimize your own ship's damage.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Maintaining Pirate Cohesion">

Cohesion is the lifeblood of your operation. It represents the unity of your crew. If this bar drops to zero, the game ends.

| Event Type | Effect on Cohesion | Mitigation Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Low Food/Booze** | Constant drain | Build more galleys/taverns |
| **Internal Feuds** | Sudden drop | Spend coin to settle disputes |
| **Ship Attacks** | Massive drop | Maintain defensive gun towers |
| **Successful Raids** | Moderate gain | Complete exploration objectives |
