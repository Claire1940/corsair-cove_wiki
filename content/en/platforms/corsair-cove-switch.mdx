| Category | Primary Goods | Purpose |

<Callout type="info" title="Logistics Insight">
The "Fetchers" are the backbone of your economy. They move goods from production buildings to distribution hubs. If your "Cohesion" is dropping, check if your Fetchers are stuck in long, red-colored logistics paths.
</Callout>

Players must balance local production with "Off-Island Supply." Some resources, like Grain or Whale Blubber, cannot be produced on the starting island and must be raided or traded for on the high seas.

  <Step num="1" title="Preparation">

  <Step num="2" title="Event Matching">
