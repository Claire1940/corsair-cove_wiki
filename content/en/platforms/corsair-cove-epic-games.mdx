<Section icon={Map} title="Settlement Layout and Resource Management">

Building a pirate empire requires a steady flow of raw materials and finished goods. Players must establish efficient production lines to convert raw timber and iron into advanced ship components and weapons. The placement of storage yards relative to production facilities determines the overall efficiency of your settlement. Minimizing transport times between the harvesting nodes and processing facilities prevents economic bottlenecks.

| Resource | Source | Primary Use | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Timber** | Logging Camp | Basic construction and ship repair | High |
| **Rations** | Fish Monger / Farm | Crew upkeep and morale maintenance | Critical |
| **Iron** | Surface Mine | Weapon crafting and advanced upgrades | Medium |
| **Rum** | Distillery | Keeping the crew happy and motivated | High |

Each building requires regular maintenance and labor. If production lines break down, raw materials pile up while advanced structures run out of components. Balancing the supply chain is critical to preventing bottlenecks in your economy. Upgrading production buildings increases their output but also raises their resource consumption, requiring a careful balancing act.

| Building | Cost | Output | Upkeep |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shantytown Hub** | 100 Timber | Population capacity +10 | 10 Rations |
| **Fish Monger** | 50 Timber | 15 Rations per cycle | 2 Gold |
| **Distillery** | 150 Timber, 20 Iron | 10 Rum per cycle | 5 Rations |
| **Shipyard** | 300 Timber, 100 Iron | Ship construction access | 15 Gold |

<Callout type="warning" title="Supply Bottlenecks">
Avoid placing storage yards too far from production buildings, as transport times can severely delay construction projects.
</Callout>

Expanding your territory allows access to rare resources like gold ore and exotic spices. These high-tier items are essential for trading with neutral ports and unlocking advanced technologies in the late game. Protecting these transport routes from rival factions is vital for maintaining a stable economy.
