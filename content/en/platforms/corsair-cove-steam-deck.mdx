<Section icon={Settings} title="Best Graphics Settings for 2026">

To achieve the best **Corsair Cove Steam Deck** experience, you must balance the artistic pirate aesthetic with the technical limitations of the handheld. The following settings are optimized to reduce GPU heat while keeping the "Seven Seas" looking vibrant and detailed.

| Setting | Recommended Value | Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Resolution** | 1280x800 (Native) | High - Keeps text sharp |
| **Texture Quality** | Medium/High | Medium - VRAM dependent |
| **Shadow Quality** | Low | High - Huge FPS gain |
| **Water Reflections** | Medium | Medium - Essential for sea vibe |
| **Anti-Aliasing** | FSR 2.0 (Quality) | High - Smooths jagged edges |

<Callout type="tip" title="FSR Usage">

</Callout>

<Section icon={Gamepad2} title="Optimizing Controls and Navigation">

Since Corsair Cove was designed with a mouse and keyboard in mind, navigating your pirate haven on a handheld requires a smart controller setup. The Steam Deck's trackpads are your greatest asset here, allowing for precise placement of buildings and management of crew happiness.

**Recommended Controller Mappings:**

| Steam Deck Button | Game Action | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Right Trackpad** | Mouse Movement | Precise building placement |
| **Left Trackpad** | Radial Menu (Hotkeys) | Quick access to Build/Map/Crews |
| **L4 / R4 Grip** | Zoom In / Out | Better view of production lines |
| **L5 / R5 Grip** | Cycle Units | Manage crew members quickly |
| **Right Trigger** | Left Mouse Click | Primary interaction |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enable Trackpad Mouse">
    Go to Controller Settings > Edit Layout. Set the Right Trackpad to "Mouse" with a medium sensitivity for smooth settlement navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Configure Radial Menus">
    Assign the Left Trackpad to a Virtual Menu. Map the numbers 1-5 to this menu to quickly swap between building categories and the exploration map.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Adjust Deadzones">
    Set your joystick deadzones to 10% to prevent "drift" when sailing your mighty ships across the ocean.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Hammer} title="Settlement Management on the Go">

Building your pirate empire in **Corsair Cove Steam Deck** requires constant attention to resource production and crew morale. On a smaller screen, it is easy to miss notifications regarding resource shortages or unhappy pirates.

| Resource Type | Management Tip | Handheld Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Timber** | Keep camps near forests | High - Core building block |
| **Rum** | Automate production early | Critical - Keeps crew happy |
| **Iron** | Focus on coastal mines | Medium - Needed for ships |
| **Gold** | Set up trade routes | Low - Late game focus |

**Production Line Efficiency:**
- **Grouping**: Place your Rum distilleries near your Sugar plantations to minimize walking distance for your crew.
- **Storage**: Ensure every 3-4 production buildings have a dedicated warehouse nearby to prevent bottlenecks.
- **Upgrades**: Use the Steam Deck's "Steam + L1" magnifier to read the small upgrade requirement text on specialized buildings.
