<Section icon={Swords} title="Corsair Cove Where Are the Attack Points">

The most common question for players is identifying the exact "attack points" where combat efficiency is maximized. In Corsair Cove, these points are divided between the sunny beaches and the dark depths of the dungeon. The beach is dominated by Sand Crabs, which are the gold standard for low-level AFK training due to their high HP and non-existent defensive stats.

<Section icon={Target} title="Dungeon Attack Points and Mechanics">

For players looking for profit alongside experience, the Corsair Cove Dungeon is the superior choice. The "attack points" here are defined by safe-spotting locations rather than multi-aggro tiles. Ogresses are large, slow, and easily trapped behind rocks or pillars, making them perfect for Ranged or Magic training.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter the Hole">
    Locate the dungeon entrance in the northwest section of the cove. You must have completed the "The Corsair Curse" quest to enter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Safe-Spots">
    In the main chamber, look for the stalagmites. Position yourself so the Ogress is on the opposite side.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Focus Target">
    Prioritize Ogress Shamans for the best loot-to-effort ratio. They drop Law runes and Nature runes frequently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Loot and Alch">
    Use High Level Alchemy on Rune and Adamant drops to extend your trip indefinitely without needing to bank.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| **Shaman** | Ranged | Yes | 50+ Ranged |
