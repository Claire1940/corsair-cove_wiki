Implementing specialized layout patterns will help you minimize travel times and prevent traffic congestion across different elevation levels.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Production Chains and Logistics Networks">

The logistics system uses color-coded route lines to indicate efficiency. Select any production building to inspect its transport lines: green indicates a short, healthy route; yellow represents a suboptimal path; and red warns of a severe bottleneck that will stall production.

| Provisions | Fish, Grain, Water | Hard Tack, Stew | Ale, Rum | Crew upkeep and Pirate Camp satisfaction |
