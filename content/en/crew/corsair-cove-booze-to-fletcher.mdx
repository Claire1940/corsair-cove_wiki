<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Setting Up the Booze Production Chain">

To satisfy Fletcher's demanding thirst, you must establish a reliable beverage production network. In Corsair Cove, alcohol is divided into different tiers, with Ale serving as the basic option and Rum representing the preferred high-tier luxury. Producing these beverages requires a careful balance of farming, water gathering, and processing.

Ale production is the easiest starting point. It requires Grain and Clean Water. Grain must be cultivated on flat, fertile soil, which is often scarce near the shoreline. You will need to build Grain Farms on the lower terraces and connect them to Water Collectors. Once harvested, these raw materials must be routed to a Brewery, where Production Workers will process them into Ale. 

Rum production is more complex but highly favored by Fletcher. It requires Sugar and Clean Water. Because sugar cane does not grow naturally on the starting island, you must either import sugar through your Dock or launch successful raids on nearby tropical islands using your fleet. Once sugar is secured, route it to a Distillery to produce Rum.

| Beverage | Raw Materials | Production Building | Output Rate | Crew Satisfaction |
| Ale | Grain, Clean Water | Brewery | High | Basic Crew |
| Rum | Sugar, Clean Water | Distillery | Medium | Specialist Crew |
| Wine | Imported Grapes | Advanced Winery | Low | Elite Crew |

<Callout type="tip" title="Production Tip">
Always build your Water Collectors on higher ground. Gravity-fed connections do not require additional energy, reducing the overall operational cost of your Breweries and Distilleries.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="Optimizing Logistics: Fetchers and Route Efficiency">

Once your Breweries and Distilleries are operational, the next challenge is transporting the booze to Fletcher's remote cliffside camp. This is where your logistics network and Fetchers come into play. Fetchers are assigned pirates responsible for moving goods between production buildings, distribution hubs, and residential camps.

Because Corsair Cove features extreme vertical terrain, pathfinding can quickly become a major bottleneck. If your Fetchers have to walk up winding mountain paths to deliver the alcohol, the transit time will be too long, resulting in a red-distance route. Red routes indicate severe logistical inefficiency, which causes delivery delays and can lead to a drop in Fletcher's satisfaction. To prevent this, you must utilize advanced vertical infrastructure.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Delivery Guide">

To successfully complete the delivery and secure Fletcher's services, follow this optimized progression route.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Fletcher's Outpost">
    Explore the northern ridge of your island to locate Fletcher's camp. Once discovered, build a road connection from your main settlement toward the base of his cliff to prepare for infrastructure placement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Construct the Water and Grain Network">
    Place two Grain Farms on a fertile terrace and support them with at least three Water Collectors. Ensure these buildings are connected to a localized warehouse to store the raw materials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Build and Supply the Brewery">
    Construct a Brewery near your warehouse. Assign Production Workers to the Brewery and monitor the resource panel to ensure the inflow of Grain and Water matches the Brewery's consumption rate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Establish a Vertical Zipline Connection">
    Unlock Ziplines through your Pirate Principles menu. Place a Zipline sender station next to your Brewery and the receiver station directly inside Fletcher's camp operating radius.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Assign Dedicated Fetchers">
    Assign at least two Fetchers to the Zipline station. They will automatically load the produced Ale or Rum onto the Zipline, delivering the booze directly to Fletcher in seconds and completing the quest.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Quest Completed">
Once Fletcher's thirst is satisfied, his workshop blueprint will unlock. Place the Fletcher's Workshop near your harbor to begin crafting advanced cannons and defensive ammunition.
