<Section icon={Map} title="Unlocking Shipwright Soraya in Corsair Cove">

Shipwright Soraya (NPC ID 15373) is a vital non-player character located at the southern port of Corsair Cove. As the primary **corsair cove shipwright**, she plays a central role in the progression of the Sailing skill, allowing players to upgrade their vessels from basic rafts to advanced sloops. However, players cannot access her full suite of services immediately upon arrival. 

To utilize her upgrading capabilities, you must meet specific skill and quest milestones. Without these prerequisites, Soraya's utility is severely limited, offering only the option to dismantle or destroy your current watercraft.

| Requirement | Type | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Level 40 Sailing** | Skill | Required to dock at the port and access customization options |
| **The Corsair Cove** | Quest | Must be completed to unlock full NPC interactions in the area |
| **Docked Vessel** | Mechanic | Your boat must be docked at the Corsair Cove port |

<Callout type="warning" title="Access Restriction">
Attempting to speak with Soraya regarding upgrades before reaching Level 40 Sailing or completing the required quest will result in a dialogue loop where she only offers to destroy your active boat. Ensure your stats are updated before traveling to the cove.
</Callout>

Accessing Corsair Cove itself can be achieved via the charter ship system or through the port south of Feldip Hills. Once the area is unlocked, Soraya can be found standing near the docks, adjacent to the shipbuilding platform.

The upgrade system categorizes vessels into three primary tiers. Each tier requires a different base hull design and offers varying stats for speed, durability, and crew capacity.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Core Shipbuilding Components and Materials">

Upgrading your ship is not merely cosmetic; it directly affects your performance during Sailing activities. Soraya allows you to upgrade four core structural components: Hulls, Helms, Masts/Sails, and Keels. Higher-tier materials increase your vessel's resistance to environmental hazards and improve navigation speeds.

Hulls determine the overall health of your ship, while Helms and Keels improve turning rates and stability in rough waters. Masts and sails dictate your acceleration and top speed.

| Component | Available Tiers | Key Materials Required |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Hulls** | Wooden, Oak, Teak, Mahogany, Camphor, Ironwood, Rosewood | Planks, Nails, Tar |
| **Helms** | Bronze, Iron, Steel, Mithril, Adamant, Rune, Dragon | Metal bars, Oak planks |
| **Keels** | Bronze, Iron, Steel, Mithril, Adamant, Rune, Dragon | Heavy metal plates, Bolts |
| **Masts & Sails** | Wooden/Linen, Oak/Linen, Teak/Canvas, Mahogany/Canvas, Rosewood/Cotton | Logs, Canvas, Cotton cloth |

<Callout type="info" title="Material Sourcing">
High-tier hulls like Ironwood and Rosewood require rare timber that must be processed using the Woodcutting and Construction skills before bringing them to the shipwright.
</Callout>

Upgrading these core parts requires a sequential progression. For example, you cannot install a Rune Helm directly onto a basic Raft; the hull must first be upgraded to a Sloop to support the weight of higher-tier metal components.

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Boat Modification Process">

Modifying your vessel at the Corsair Cove docks follows a strict procedural loop. Follow these steps to ensure your materials are processed correctly without losing progress.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Verify Requirements">
    Confirm you have reached Level 40 Sailing and completed The Corsair Cove quest. Ensure your ship is docked at the southern pier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Materials">
    Withdraw the necessary planks, metal bars, and sails from your bank. Keep them in your inventory before speaking to the NPC.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Customization">
    Speak to Shipwright Soraya and select the **\"Customise-boat\"** option. You will be transitioned to the shipyard interface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Apply Upgrades">
    Select the desired component category (Hull, Helm, Mast, or Keel) and confirm the upgrade. The materials will be consumed automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Deconstruction Warning">
Selecting the option to destroy your boat will permanently delete all applied upgrades and return only a fraction of the raw materials. Use this option only if you intend to rebuild a completely different class of vessel.
</Callout>

</Section>
