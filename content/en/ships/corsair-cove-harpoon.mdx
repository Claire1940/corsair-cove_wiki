<Section icon={Map} title="Unlocking the Corsair Cove Resource Area">

The **Corsair Cove harpoon** fishing spots are located within a gated, resource-rich zone in the northwest corner of Corsair Cove. This area was specifically designed to provide high-level Free-to-Play (F2P) players with a viable alternative to the crowded docks of Musa Point on Karamja. However, unlike the public docks, this area requires significant progression to access.

To enter the gated area, players must have completed the legendary quest, **Dragon Slayer I**. Once the quest is finished, you need to find an NPC named **Haris**, who stands guard at the entrance. Speaking to him after your victory over Elvarg will grant you permanent access to the surface and underground portions of the cove.

| Requirement | Type | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Dragon Slayer I** | Quest | Must be fully completed |
| **Haris** | NPC | Speak to him at the gate to unlock |
| **The Corsair Curse** | Quest | Required to travel to the Cove initially |
| **Fishing Level 35** | Skill | Required for Harpoon (Tuna) |
| **Fishing Level 50** | Skill | Required for Harpoon (Swordfish) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
If you are already inside the Corsair Cove Dungeon, you can also access the resource area through a gate in the northern section of the cave, provided you have spoken to Haris above ground first.

<Section icon={Target} title="Fishing Mechanics and Efficiency">

The primary draw for many players is the pier located on the western edge of the resource area. There are **3 harpoon/cage fishing spots** available here. These spots allow players to catch Raw Tuna and Raw Swordfish using a harpoon, or Raw Lobster using a lobster pot. 

In the current 2026 meta, these spots are preferred over Karamja because the pier is significantly quieter, meaning the fishing spots move less frequently due to lower player density. This results in more "AFK" (Away From Keyboard) time, making it an excellent choice for players multi-tasking or looking for a relaxed experience.

| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Raw Tuna** | 35 | 80 | Harpoon |
| **Raw Swordfish** | 50 | 100 | Harpoon |
| **Raw Lobster** | 40 | 90 | Lobster Pot |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Navigating the Resource Area Dangers">

While the pier itself is a safe zone, the path to the **corsair cove harpoon** spots and the surrounding resources are guarded by powerful Ogresses. Ogress Warriors and Ogress Shamans roam the northern part of the resource area and the dungeon below. 

If you are a low-level player who rushed Dragon Slayer I, be extremely careful when walking from the gate to the pier. The Ogresses are aggressive and can hit quite hard with both melee and magic.

| Enemy | Level | Combat Style | Aggressive? |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Ogress Warrior** | 82 | Melee | Yes |
| **Ogress Shaman** | 82 | Magic | Yes |
| **Captain Tock** | N/A | NPC (Friendly) | No |

<Callout type="info" title="Safety First">
The fishing pier is far enough south that the Ogresses will not attack you while you are fishing. The danger only exists during the transit from the gate or the dungeon entrance to the water's edge.
</Callout>
