<Section icon={Settings} title="Ship Upgrades and Resource Requirements">

Upgrading your **corsair cove ships** is a multi-stage process that involves both material costs and time. The Tier 2 upgrade path introduces the need for **Ropes**, a resource produced by the Rope Maker using fibers from leaf cutters. Without a steady supply of ropes, your fleet will remain grounded in Tier 1.

| Ship Model | Class | Primary Material | Secondary Material | Key Advantage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tide Runner** | Hunter | 15 Ropes | 10 Planks | High Maneuverability |
| **Steel Edge** | Destroyer | 25 Ropes | 20 Planks | Massive Gun Damage |
| **Iron Fist** | Heavy | 30 Ropes | 25 Planks | Maximum Hull Health |
| **Shark Tooth** | Reaver | 20 Ropes | 15 Planks | Balanced Boarding |

To facilitate these upgrades, your production camps must be interconnected. If your Rope Maker is located in a secondary camp, ensure that the transport routes to the main shipyard are optimized. The use of elevators and pathways is essential to keep the flow of planks and ropes consistent during the construction phase.

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Bottlenecks">
The consumption of **Planks** increases exponentially as your city grows. Before starting a Tier 2 ship upgrade, pause non-essential construction to ensure the shipwright has priority access to the lumber supply.
</Callout>

Maintaining crew **Cohesion** is vital. If your supplies of Bread or Rum run out, cohesion will drop, leading to reduced ship performance or even mutiny. Cohesion at 100% provides hidden bonuses to mission success rates, making it worthwhile to invest in the "Salty Cabins" for better housing.

<Section icon={Zap} title="Step-by-Step: Launching Your First Tier 2 Vessel">

The process of commissioning a Tier 2 ship requires careful planning of your island's infrastructure. Follow these steps to ensure a smooth launch without crashing your local economy.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Rope Production">
    Build a Rope Maker near your leaf cutters. Ensure the output is linked to a warehouse that is accessible by your main shipyard. You will need at least 15-20 ropes for the initial upgrade.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Upgrade to Salty Cabins">
    Before recruiting Scallywags, upgrade your basic tents to Salty Cabins. These require ropes and planks but provide the necessary comfort for higher-tier crew members.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initialize the Bread Chain">
    Construct a Cassava Picker on fertile land, a Salt Mine, and an Oven. Use a **Zipline** to transport fresh bread directly to the Galley to minimize transport time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Commission the Ship">
    Select your docked vessel and choose the Tier 2 upgrade (e.g., Tide Runner). Assign a Captain with high maneuverability for exploration or high damage for combat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Assign specialized Crew">
    Fill the crew slots with a mix of Swabbies and Scallywags. Ensure your Galley has a surplus of Bread before the ship leaves port to prevent cohesion loss at sea.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Optimization Tip">
Use the **Area Lift** to connect high-elevation production zones (like cliff-side Cassava farms) to your coastal warehouses. This drastically reduces the "walking time" of your carriers.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="Naval Missions and Exploration Rewards">

Once your **corsair cove ships** are battle-ready, you can venture into new regions like the **Ancient Archipelago**. These missions are categorized into four types: Wealth, Fighter, Explorer, and Empire. Each type requires different ship stats to maximize the loot.

- **Wealth Missions**: Focus on cargo capacity and trade routes.
- **Fighter Missions**: Require high gun damage and boarding stats.
- **Explorer Missions**: Prioritize speed and maneuverability to navigate fogs and storms.
- **Empire Missions**: Aim to build alliances with local tribes and buccaneers.

| Mission Location | Difficulty | Required Stat | Key Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Ancient Archipelago | Tier 2 | Maneuverability | 80 Drifters |
| Great Pyramid | Tier 2 | Gun Damage | Ancient Treasure |
| Spanish Hound Prowl | Tier 2 | Boarding | Principal Points |
| Friendly Port | Tier 1 | Speed | Ship Repairs |

During exploration, you will encounter "Discovery Events." Building a **Crow's Nest** in your settlement provides a 20-gold bonus for every event discovered, making it a lucrative passive income stream for aggressive explorers.

<Callout type="info" title="The Tribe Alliance">
Helping the Tribe Alliance in the Ancient Archipelago can grant you **Empire Principal Points**. These points are used to unlock advanced buildings like the Gambling Den or the Trading Post.
</Callout>
