<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Progression Roadmap">

To fully master the maritime potential of this region, follow this structured progression path. This ensures you unlock all shortcuts and maximize your XP gains per hour.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Quest Foundation">
    Start and complete **The Corsair Curse**. This unlocks the bank, the notice board, and the ability to dock your ship at the main pier. Without this, your Sailing utility is severely limited.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Skill Integration">
    Achieve level 40 Sailing to begin accepting port tasks. Simultaneously, aim for 30 Agility and complete Dragon Slayer I to gain access to the Resource Area via Haris.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Task Optimization">
    Focus on **Hammerhead Shark** bounties. The 8,800 XP reward is one of the highest for its level bracket. Combine this with courier runs to Port Khazard for a balanced XP/Profit ratio.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Advanced Construction">
    Once you reach 51 Sailing, build the rowboat at Angler's Retreat. This creates a permanent high-speed link that is essential for late-game resource farming and task cycling.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Combat Synergy">
While waiting for notice board resets, spend time in the Corsair Cove Dungeon. The ogress shamans provide excellent magic and ranged XP, and their drops can fund your Sailing supplies.
</Callout>
