<Section icon={Map} title="Locating the Corsair Cove Rowboat Shortcut">

The **corsair cove rowboat** is a specialized transport amenity introduced with the expansion of the Mythic Sea in late 2025. Located southwest of the main Corsair Cove docks, this unassuming vessel provides a direct link to Anglers' Retreat, an island otherwise restricted to high-level sailors. Understanding the geography of this region is crucial for players looking to optimize their travel routes in the southern oceans.

Before you can utilize the rowboat from the Corsair Cove side, you must first reach Anglers' Retreat via traditional Sailing. The island is situated south of the Myths' Guild and north of the Isle of Bones. The waters here are notoriously difficult to navigate due to the "fetid" status of the sea, which can damage unprepared vessels and players.

| Feature | Details |
| --- | --- |
| **Mainland Location** | Southwest of Corsair Cove docks |
| **Island Destination** | Anglers' Retreat (Mythic Sea) |
| **Sailing Requirement** | Level 51 (Unboostable) |
| **Prerequisite** | Build the rowboat amenity on the island |
| **Hazards** | Fetid waters, high Sailing level requirement |

<Callout type="warning" title="Navigation Warning">
While it is technically possible to navigate the western edge of the fetid waters without an inoculation station, it is extremely difficult. Most players should ensure their ship is properly furnished before attempting the initial mooring at Anglers' Retreat.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Construction and Material Requirements">

Unlocking the **corsair cove rowboat** is not as simple as clicking a prompt. It requires a specific set of Construction levels and raw materials. Because Anglers' Retreat is a remote location, players are advised to bring all necessary items in a single trip to avoid multiple dangerous voyages through the Mythic Sea.

To build all amenities on the island, including the rowboat, you will need a total of 21 inventory slots. However, this can be significantly reduced by using specialized equipment such as the Imcando hammer or Amy's saw. The following table outlines the specific materials required for the primary utilities found on the island.

| Utility | Construction Level | Materials Required | Experience Gained |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Rowboat (Amenity)** | 24 | 2 Oak planks, 2 Iron bars, Hammer, Saw | 141 |
| **Cooking Pot** | 19 | 2 Logs, 2 Iron bars, Tinderbox, Hammer, Saw | 23 |
| **Spinning Wheel** | 24 | 2 Oak planks, 2 Iron bars, Ball of wool, Hammer, Saw | 141 |

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Construction Guide">

Setting up your permanent link via the **corsair cove rowboat** involves a specific progression. You cannot build the mainland side; the "Rowboat (amenity)" must be constructed on the island of Anglers' Retreat first. Follow these steps to ensure a successful setup.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prepare Your Vessel">
    Ensure your ship has at least Level 51 Sailing capability. Install an inoculation station at a port to protect against the fetid waters of the Mythic Sea.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Materials">
    Collect 4 Oak planks, 4 Iron bars, 2 Logs, and a Ball of wool. Don't forget your hammer, saw, and tinderbox. This covers all island amenities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initial Voyage">
    Depart from the Myths' Guild or a nearby port and sail south. Navigate to the mooring point on the north side of Anglers' Retreat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Build the Rowboat">
    Locate the rowboat ghost-node on the southwestern shore of the island. Interact with it using 2 Oak planks and 2 Iron bars to complete the shortcut.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Verify the Shortcut">
    Use the newly built rowboat to travel back to Corsair Cove. The rowboat south-west of the cove will now be fully functional for two-way travel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Unlock Complete">
Once Step 5 is finished, you no longer need Level 51 Sailing or a specialized boat to access Anglers' Retreat. You can simply walk to the Corsair Cove docks and take the rowboat.
</Callout>

</Section>
