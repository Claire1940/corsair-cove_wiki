<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Earn Compass Points">
    Complete deeds and events on the world map to earn specialized progression points. These points are divided into Notoriety, Seafaring, Wealth, and Empire categories. Focus on a single path early on to accelerate your high-tier ship unlocks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock the Ship Blueprint">
    Open the Compass menu and navigate to your chosen path. Spend your earned points to unlock the specific ship node. For example, unlocking the Tier 2 Moonlight ship requires investing points into the early nodes of the Notoriety or Seafaring trees.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Construct a Pier">
    Select a coastal node on your island and construct a Pier. Ensure the Pier has a direct road or ladder connection to your warehouse and residential districts so your pirate workers can transport building materials efficiently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deliver Construction Materials">
    Select the Pier and choose the unlocked ship blueprint you wish to build. Your fetchers will automatically begin transporting planks, rope, and coins from your warehouses to the Pier. Ensure your sawmills are running at maximum capacity during this phase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Assign Captain and Crew">
    Once construction is complete, select your new ship at the Pier. Assign an unlocked captain (such as Scarlet or Blackbeard) and recruit the required number of crew members using beer from your local tavern to officially launch the vessel.
  </Step>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Compass Progression Paths & Earning Deeds">

The Compass is the heart of your progression in Corsair Cove. It acts as a multi-path tech tree that dictates what buildings, resources, and ships you can access. To earn the points required to climb these trees, you must complete specific milestones known as Deeds, or participate in dynamic events scattered across the world map.

Understanding which path aligns with your desired gameplay style is crucial for optimizing your ship progression. The table below details the four Compass paths, their associated playstyles, and the types of deeds required to unlock them.

| Compass Path | Associated Playstyle | Key Deed Example | Primary Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Notoriety** | Pirate Population & Fame | Reach 500 total pirate population | Unlocks fast, high-capacity boarding ships |
| **Seafaring** | Naval Combat & Interception | Ambush 5 Crown merchant ships | Unlocks heavily armored combat vessels |
| **Wealth** | Trade, Smuggling & Economy | Accumulate 1000 gold coins | Unlocks high-capacity cargo and trade ships |
| **Empire** | Island Defense & Order | Build 3 small gun towers | Unlocks defensive structures and hybrid ships |

To unlock your first major ship upgrade, the Tier 2 Moonlight, focusing on the Notoriety path is highly recommended. Achieving a high pirate population is a natural byproduct of expanding your settlement, making Notoriety points the easiest to accumulate in the early game.
