<Section icon={Map} title="Advanced Island Infrastructure and Logistics">

A powerful navy is entirely dependent on a stable domestic economy. To support a continuous military campaign, your island infrastructure must efficiently produce food, water, and luxury goods to keep your workforce satisfied. The primary bottleneck in the mid-game is managing your drifter population, as operating advanced production buildings requires a constant influx of labor.

Salt and bread production form the core of your community's sustenance. Salt deposits are often located on high plateaus, making manual transportation via ladders incredibly slow. Implementing mechanical transportation systems, such as elevators and ziplines, drastically speeds up resource transit, ensuring your bakeries receive a steady supply of salt to maintain bread production.

  <Step num="1" title="Locate Salt Deposits">

  <Step num="2" title="Build Basic Housing">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Install Vertical Elevators">
    Connect the plateau to the lowland warehouse using a two-way mechanical elevator.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Establish Bridge Connections">
    Build direct bridges over water obstacles to minimize the walking distance for your drifters.
  </Step>
</Steps>
